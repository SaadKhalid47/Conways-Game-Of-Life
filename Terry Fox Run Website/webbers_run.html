Information

This year’s Webber Academy Terry Fox event will be held on Thursday, September 26, 2020. Terry Fox was a remarkable individual and his legacy continues to inspire countless people worldwide. The positive qualities Terry possessed are visible in many of our terrific students. Being a part of Terry Fox’s legacy, through raising funds and awareness for cancer research, is an honour we hold very dear at our school. During a class with this year’s Physical Education 30


Mission

Since 1998, Webber Academy has raised over $610,000.00 for the Terry Fox Foundation and cancer research. Our goal this year is to raise $35, 000.00. This is indeed an optimistic goal; however, with your support and generosity, we are confident that together, we can meet this challenge. We would like to extend an invitation to parents who wish to join us on the day of the run. It is the strength of our community bond that truly makes this event so special. Donation forms have been sent home with each Webber Academy student and we encourage everyone to support this great cause. We will continue to collect donations until Monday, October, 18, 2019.
