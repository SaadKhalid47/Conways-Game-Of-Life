Message From The Owner

Hi my name is Giovanni Francesco and I was born in Rome, Italy. A few years ago I realized that I was a celiac. Celiac disease, sometimes called celiac sprue or gluten-sensitive enteropathy, is an immune reaction to eating gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley and rye. However whenever I went to eat out with friends and family, it was difficult to find gluten free meals and goods. As a result I decided to make my own gluten free restaurant that sticks to my Italian roots. As my grandma used to say "italian food is all about ingredients and it's not fussy and its not fancy." Thats why we bake fresh food everyday and only use the highest quality of ingredients from local farmers. Mozzafiato is an italian name for breathtaking and this is what we want you to feel when you enter our restaurant.



Mozafiato is pleased to partner with the Canadian Celiac Association (CCA) in bringing you 100% gluten free food. We take the health and saftey of our customers very seriously and do not cross contaminate. We are proud to have won numerous awards and have one the trip advisor award for best restaurant in Calgary.
